About Hydro-M

Advanced Waterway & Level Monitoring System

Powered by UEGCL

Our Mission

Under the ownership of UEGCL, Hydro-M is a comprehensive waterway and level monitoring system designed to extract valuable insights into how climatic conditions affect hydro power generation across the cascade.

Advanced Technology

Hydro-M leverages cutting-edge machine learning algorithms to make accurate predictions of water levels in dam reservoirs along the CASCADE. Our system provides real-time monitoring of cascaded dam activities including dispatch and discharge.

Key Features

  • Real-time water level monitoring
  • Predictive analytics using ML
  • Cascade dam activity tracking
  • Climate impact analysis
  • Dispatch & discharge monitoring
  • Access AI-Powered system generated reports and analytics
