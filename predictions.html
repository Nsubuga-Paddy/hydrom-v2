Understanding Performance Metrics

MAE
Mean Absolute Error measures the average magnitude of prediction errors in millimeters.
Our Result: ±2.3mm means predictions are typically within 2.3mm of actual values - excellent precision for water level forecasting.
RMSE
Root Mean Square Error gives higher weight to larger prediction errors, providing insight into worst-case scenarios.
Our Result: ±3.1mm indicates that even larger errors are well-controlled and within acceptable engineering tolerances.
R² Score
R-squared measures how well the model explains the variance in water levels, ranging from 0 to 1.
Our Result: 0.94 means the model explains 94% of water level variations - indicating very high predictive power.
24h Accuracy
Percentage of 24-hour predictions that fall within acceptable error margins for operational decision-making.
Our Result: 94.2% accuracy provides high confidence for daily operational planning and risk management.
